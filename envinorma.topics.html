envinorma.topics.simple_topics module¶

Generate very simple topics for AMs.

envinorma.topics.simple_topics.add_simple_topics(am: envinorma.models.arrete_ministeriel.ArreteMinisteriel) → envinorma.models.arrete_ministeriel.ArreteMinisteriel[source]¶

Add simple topics to AM sections.

We first look for the depth in the am structure at which at least 3 titles match the ontology. Then we detect topics for all titles at this depth.

Args:

am (ArreteMinisteriel): arrete ministeriel to decorate.

Returns:

ArreteMinisteriel: arrete ministeriel with simple topics where detected.
